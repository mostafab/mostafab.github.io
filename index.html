About Me

Hi there! I'm Mostafa Bhuiyan.

I am currently a junior at the Georgia Institute of Technology pursuing a Bachelors of Science degree in Computer Science. My concentrations deal with Information Internetworks and People because I would like to pursue full-stack web development as a career, and these two concentrations best enable me to pursue this dream of mine. Getting to combine the ability to create beatiful, intuitive interfaces for end-users in the front-end and the unique puzzles a back-end developer comes across is one of the most satisfying things I've experienced in my time in web development.

Perhaps most importantly, given the fact that web development is one of the fastest moving areas in computer science, I know that I will always be learning something new in full-stack web development. The popular frameworks of today may not be the go to solutions for tomorrow. I believe this characteristic will enable me to focus more on the bigger picture behind the problems software developers face and the principles they apply instead of the ins and outs of any particular framework, which in turn will make me a better developer.

Interests

When I'm not programming, I enjoy playing basketball and discovering new music, particularly jazz music!
